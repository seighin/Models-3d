Original:
This is a riser for the common Rosewill/Best/Flexzion/Yobo food dehydrators.  It holds 2 1KG spools.  With this mod, you don't need to destroy your food trays to fit a spool.   This dehydrator sells for about one third the price of the exact same filament dryer unit.   They are two versions; a three and a four-part unit.  The three-part panel is quite large.   I've also included a 4 part STL including two of the 4 needed panels.   It will print on a typical 8.4" bed.  Ive included .5mm clearence on the pin shaft.   If thats not enough you can scale the pins just in the x-y directions or drill out the holes a little.  The pins are optional, you can substitute bolts or even zip ties.
